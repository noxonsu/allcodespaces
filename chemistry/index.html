Case Studies

Success Stories

AI Discovers New Antibiotic

Goal: Discover a new antibiotic capable of killing drug-resistant bacteria.

Method: Deep learning model analyzed over 100 million chemical structures to predict antibacterial activity.

Result: Discovery of Halicin, a new antibiotic that successfully kills multiple strains of multi-drug-resistant bacteria, representing a new class of antibacterial compounds.

AI-Designed Drug for Fibrosis

Goal: Develop a new drug for idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) using AI.

Method: Integrated AI platform identified novel disease target and generated active compounds against that target.

Result: Creation of Rentosertib, which successfully passed preclinical tests and entered clinical trials, becoming one of the first medications developed by modern AI in just 18 months.

AI-Driven Drug Repurposing (COVID-19)

Goal: Quickly find an existing drug that could treat COVID-19.

Method: AI knowledge graph platform mapped SARS-CoV-2 infection mechanisms to a database of approved drugs.

Result: Identification of Baricitinib (rheumatoid arthritis drug) as effective COVID-19 treatment, later incorporated into treatment guidelines and reducing mortality in patients.
